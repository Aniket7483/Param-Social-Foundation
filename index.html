About Param Social Foundation

Established in 2018, Param Social Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organization dedicated to empowering rural, tribal, and underprivileged communities across India through sustainable development initiatives.

We focus on measurable impact through government partnerships, CSR initiatives, and community-driven programs in education, skill development, healthcare, women empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

Key Achievements:
  • Trained 12,500+ youth in vocational skills
  • Empowered 5,000+ women through SHGs
  • Covered 1,000+ villages across 29 states
  • Placed 1,000+ beneficiaries in jobs

Our Programs

Detailed initiatives driving sustainable change in communities.

Vocational Skill Training

Comprehensive training programs under DDUGKY and PMKVY schemes, equipping youth with skills in tailoring, electronics, hospitality, and more. Over 12,500 youth trained and placed in jobs.

  • Duration: 6-12 months
  • Placement assistance
  • Industry partnerships

Women Empowerment through SHGs

Formation and strengthening of Self-Help Groups for women, focusing on financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and capacity building. Empowered 5,000+ women across rural areas.

  • Microfinance support
  • Entrepreneurship training
  • Market linkages

Health & Nutrition Camps

Mobile health camps and awareness drives for maternal and child health, immunization, and nutritional support in underserved communities.

  • Regular health check-ups
  • Nutrition education
  • Emergency medical aid
